What We Deliver

Healthcare AI Platforms

We build comprehensive digital platforms for medical institutions that automate patient aftercare, reduce physician workload, and improve treatment outcomes through intelligent technology.

Platform capabilities:

  • AI Rehabilitation Scheduling: Machine learning models analyze patient characteristics and historical treatment data to automatically generate optimal rehabilitation schedules
  • Treatment Effect Analysis: SHAP value analysis and ML models predict improvement rates, correlate patient attributes with outcomes, and quantify treatment effectiveness
  • Medical AI Chatbot: On-premise LLM provides 24/7 patient guidance on stretches, exercises, and home treatment—conversation logs integrate with electronic health records
  • Automated Communication: Scheduled check-ins, bulk messaging, and response tracking with direct database integration
  • Patient Community: Bulletin boards and messaging features to reduce isolation and support mental health recovery
  • Video & Content Delivery: Educational content with viewing history tracking for personalized AI recommendations
  • Role-Based Access: Three-tier system (patients, staff, administrators) with granular data access controls
