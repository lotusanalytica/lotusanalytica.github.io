AI-Powered Patient Aftercare Platform

2026 Healthcare Technology

A comprehensive online platform for a rehabilitation clinic specializing in lower back pain treatment. The system dramatically reduces physician workload while improving patient outcomes through intelligent automation.

Key Features:

  • AI Rehabilitation Scheduling: ML-powered automatic schedule creation based on patient characteristics and historical treatment outcomes
  • Treatment Effect Analysis: Machine learning models with SHAP value analysis to predict improvement rates and quantify treatment effectiveness
  • Medical AI Chatbot: On-premise LLM deployment for 24/7 patient guidance on exercises, stretches, and home treatment—without exposing sensitive data
  • Automated Patient Communication: Scheduled check-ins, bulk messaging, and response tracking integrated with hospital databases
  • Patient Community Platform: Bulletin boards and messaging to reduce isolation and support mental health recovery

Graph Query Performance Study

2026 Database Systems

A rigorous comparison of Regular Path Query (RPQ) execution across MySQL 8.0, MySQL 5.7, MariaDB 10.3, MongoDB 6.0, and Neo4j 5.x using the YAGO2s knowledge graph dataset with over 1 million triples.

Key finding: Native graph databases achieve 41.5x speedup over modern SQL implementations for complex path traversal queries, demonstrating the critical importance of choosing the right database architecture for graph-oriented workloads.
